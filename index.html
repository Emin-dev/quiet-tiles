How to play

  • Each pair of matching dots (same color, shape, and number) must be joined by one path.
  • Drag from a dot across adjacent tiles to draw its path. Drag back over the path to shorten it.
  • Paths can't cross or share a tile with another color.
  • The puzzle is solved when every tile is filled and every pair is connected.
  • There's no timer and no penalty for mistakes — take your time.
